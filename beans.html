Arabica Beans

Arabica beans are known for their sweet, soft taste and higher acidity. They have a complex flavor with hints of sugar, fruit, and berries. These beans are often considered superior to other varieties.

Robusta Beans

Robusta beans are stronger, harsher, and have a grain-like overtone compared to Arabica. They contain more caffeine and have a bitter, woody taste. Robusta is often used in espresso blends for its strong flavor and crema production.

Liberica Beans

Liberica beans are known for their unique, irregular shape and woody, smoky, and full-bodied flavor. They have a more earthy taste compared to Arabica and Robusta. Liberica beans are less common but offer a distinct coffee experience.
